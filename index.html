F.A.Q

  • What is a Hackathon?

    A Hackathon is a 24 hour programming event where students work together to develop a software or hardware project

  • Who can participate?

    Any High School student can particpate!

  • What if I don't know how to code?

    We will be hosting several workshops throughout the event intended to help you with your projects!

  • What should I bring?

    Laptop, charger, powerstrip, and sleeping bag are highly recommended

  • How much does it cost to participate?

    Thanks to our sponsors, this event is completely free!

  • How do I form a team?

    If you do not have a team already, there will be team formation at the beginning of the hackathon. This is an opportunity for you to meet new people and form teams. Each team should have a maximum of four people
